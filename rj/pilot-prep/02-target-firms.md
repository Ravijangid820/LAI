## Fit-scoring rubric

Each firm scored on three axes (1-5):

* **Wind energy DD volume** — do they actually run wind-park
  acquisitions, repowering deals, PPA disputes, BImSchG litigation?
* **Mid-market positioning** — too big = procurement runway is 12+
  months; too small = no budget. Sweet spot is 50-300 lawyers,
  multiple offices, willing to test tools.
* **EU AI Act sensitivity** — DPO-staffed firms with active
  compliance programs are MORE likely to value our audit-log work,
  not less.

Total /15. >10 = priority outreach; 7-10 = "if first wave fails";
<7 = skip.
