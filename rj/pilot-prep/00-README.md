## Calibration

The materials in here assume the pilot ask is:
- **Honest about our ceiling** (R@30 = 0.49, German-only val gold, lawyer-blind A/B not yet run with humans)
- **Honest about our gaps** (no formal conformity assessment, no operator kill-switch, no published model card)
- **Confident about what's real** (5 production fixes verified live this week, 1029 unit tests, audit logs satisfy EU AI Act Art. 12)

A pitch that overclaims will fail the second meeting. The 5-month
silence is partly because the boss + rj want a pitch that *won't* fail
the second meeting. These materials are written for that.

## What this kit does NOT do

* It does not pick the firm — you and boss know the German wind
  ecosystem; I don't.
* It does not draft a master services agreement or commercial
  contract — that's legal counsel's job, not mine.
* It does not promise pilot success — engineering is ready; whether
  a firm wants to be first is a sales call.
* It does not commit anyone (you, boss, or LAI) to anything. Draft
  status until you decide.
